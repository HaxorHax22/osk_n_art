Commission a Custom Abstract Artwork

Your space, your colors, your story — in abstract.

What I Create

Each commission is a bespoke abstract artwork designed specifically for your space and vision. I create pieces that capture the essence of Iceland's natural beauty while reflecting your personal color palette and aesthetic preferences.

Whether you're drawn to the golden warmth of volcanic earth, the serene grays of glacial formations, or the vibrant blues of northern seas, I translate these inspirations into abstract compositions that transform your environment.

  • Custom color palettes to match your space
  • Any size from intimate pieces to large statements
  • Choice of medium: acrylic, oil, or mixed media
  • Textured, layered compositions with depth
  • Inspiration drawn from Icelandic landscapes

How It Works

01

Choose Colors & Size

Share your color preferences, room photos, and desired dimensions. I'll provide size recommendations and pricing based on your space.

02

Consultation

We'll discuss your vision, the mood you want to create, and how the piece will interact with your existing décor and lighting.

I'll create a detailed sketch showing composition, color placement, and overall feel. We'll refine this together until it's perfect.

Your artwork comes to life over 2-4 weeks. I'll share progress photos and ensure every layer builds toward your vision.

05

Secure shipping or local delivery in Reykjavík. I'll provide installation tips and lighting suggestions to showcase your piece beautifully.

What I Need From You

To create the perfect piece for your space, I'll need a few details that help me understand your vision and environment:

Space & Dimensions

Photos of your wall/room and rough measurements of the area where the artwork will hang.

Color Inspiration

Examples of colors you love—fabric swatches, photos, or pieces from my gallery that speak to you.

Mood & Story

The feeling you want to create—energizing, calming, dramatic—and any personal stories or connections.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commission take?

Most commissions are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on size and complexity. Larger pieces may take up to 6 weeks. I'll provide a specific timeline during our consultation.

Can I see the work in progress?

Absolutely! I share progress photos at key stages and welcome your feedback throughout the creation process. This ensures the final piece perfectly matches your vision.

What if I'm not completely satisfied?

Your satisfaction is my priority. I include revision rounds in the sketch phase, and I'm committed to working with you until you're thrilled with your piece.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide with professional art packaging. Shipping costs vary by destination and are calculated separately from the artwork price.

Can you match existing artwork or décor?

I specialize in creating pieces that complement your existing space while bringing something fresh and personal. Share photos of your room and I'll design accordingly.

What materials do you use?

I work primarily with high-quality acrylics and oils on stretched canvas or wood panels. All materials are archival quality, ensuring your artwork will last for generations.
