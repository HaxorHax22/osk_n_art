Commission a Custom Abstract Artwork

Your space, your colors, your story — in abstract.

Creating Your Custom Artwork

Each commission is a bespoke abstract piece inspired by Iceland's natural beauty and designed specifically for your space. I translate the golden warmth of volcanic earth, serene glacial grays, and vibrant ocean blues into abstract compositions that transform your environment.

What I Create: Custom color palettes, any size, choice of medium (acrylic, oil, mixed media), textured layered compositions.
The Process: Initial consultation → sketch & approval → 4-8 weeks creation → delivery with installation guidance.
Your Investment: Professional-grade materials, comprehensive consultation, progress updates, and museum-quality results.

What I Need From You

To create the perfect piece for your space, I'll need a few details that help me understand your vision and environment:

Space & Dimensions

Photos of your wall/room and rough measurements of the area where the artwork will hang.

Color Inspiration

Examples of colors you love—fabric swatches, photos, or pieces from my gallery that speak to you.

Mood & Story

The feeling you want to create—energizing, calming, dramatic—and any personal stories or connections.

Budget Range

Commission investment varies by size and complexity. Share your vision and I'll propose the perfect options for your space.

My Approach to Commission Work

I create substantial abstract artworks designed to transform spaces and captivate viewers. Each piece is a significant investment in both scale and artistic impact.

Scale & Presence

My commissioned works typically range from 80×100 cm to 150×200 cm or larger, designed to command attention and create powerful focal points in sophisticated interiors.

Materials & Technique

I work exclusively with professional-grade acrylics, oils, and mixed media on premium canvas or wood panels, ensuring longevity and museum-quality results.

Timeline & Process

Each commission requires 4-8 weeks for completion, including consultation, sketch development, creation, and final finishing. I provide regular progress updates throughout the process.

Investment & Value

Each commission is individually priced based on dimensions, complexity, materials, and the unique vision we develop together. Investment typically reflects the substantial scale and artistic significance of the work.

I believe in transparent, personalized discussions about investment. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed proposal that includes the full scope of work, timeline, and investment level.

All commissions include materials, comprehensive consultation, progress documentation, professional finishing, and shipping within Iceland. International shipping and installation guidance available.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a commission take?

4-8 weeks depending on size and complexity. I'll provide a specific timeline during our consultation and share progress photos throughout the creation process.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship worldwide with professional art packaging. Shipping costs vary by destination and are calculated separately from the commission.

Can you match my existing décor?

I specialize in creating pieces that complement your space while bringing something fresh and personal. Share photos of your room and I'll design accordingly.

What if I'm not satisfied?

Your satisfaction is my priority. I include revision rounds in the sketch phase and work with you until you're thrilled with your piece.
