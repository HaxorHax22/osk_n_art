I create abstract expressions inspired by Iceland's raw beauty—the interplay of light on ancient stone, the endless textures of volcanic landscapes, and the deep blues of our northern seas. Each piece emerges from this dialogue between color and form, crafted to bring the essence of these natural forces into your space.
